What is AI ?

Artificial intelligence (AI) is wide-ranging branch of computer science concerned with building smart machines capable of performing tasks that typically require human intelligence. Every day, technology around us keeps getting smarter, from beating us in our own games to outperforming us in cognitive tasks, all of which has been made possible because of AI.

Applications of AI ?

1. Entertainment: Netflix, Amazon recommendation systems.
2. Communication: Chatbots, Personal Assistants (Siri, Cortana)
3. Gaming: Strategic games such as Chess, Go.
4. Automotive: Self-driven cars, Intelligent virtual assistants.
5. Healthcare: Better and faster diagnosis of diseases, selection of vaccine candidates.

Future of AI ?

1. Revolutionize Human Resources and Recruiting by relying on analytical data and eliminating discrimination on the basis of gender, age and race.
2. Language barriers can be permanently removed by instant speech translation.
3. Improve road safety through autonomous vehicles.
4. Increased processing powers and efficiency by incorporating Quantum Computing.

HOW A MACHINE WORKS ?

  • 7 Easy Steps of Machine Learning

  • Gathering Data

    The first pre-processing step which involves identifying and collecting relevant data.

    This is perhaps the most important step in data pre-processing and directly impacts the performance and complexity of further tasks. If you are given the task of planning the best holiday trip, the first thing you might consider doing is gathering information such as the duration, location, timing etc of the holiday. Based on this, you would collect data about possible holiday destinations matching this criteria. The more precise and relevant data you capture, the better chances of you planning the most happening holiday.

  • Preparing that Data

    Processing the data to make it ideal for processing though a machine.

    Once you have gathered the information about possible holiday destination, you can edit it to make it easy for a machine to understand it. Example: If you are capturing the address, it is best to split it up into street addrees, post code, state, country to make it easier for the machine to understand which is which.

  • Choosing a Model

    Choosing an appropriate ML model based on the objective and data.

    This can be imagined as a software where you upload the data you got and it will tell you which is the best holiday destination for you from the data.


  • After Choosing the model

  • Training of Data

    The chosen model is trained with known data to help it learn.

    For the machine to be able to help you in choosing the best holdiay destination, it is important for it to understand your preferences. During training, you tell the machine about the holiday destination you have been to and how your experience was. But don't tell the machine about all of your holidays, you must save some for the next part.

  • Evaluation

    Evaluate the model performance by feeding it data with known labels and compare the result.

    Now that you have informed the machine about your experiences, how do we know if it has actually learned? We confirm it by testing it's knowledge. Remember the few holidays about which you didn't tell the machine when it was learning? We tell those machine but instead of telling it how you experience was, we ask the machine to predict it. If the machine's prediction is same as your experience, we can assume it has learnt your pereferences.

  • Prediction

    The model now utilized what it has learnt from training data to make predictions about unseen data.

    Finally, we ask the software to tell you which of the possible destinations is the best for you so that you can pack your bags and head out for the best vacaction ever.

  • Hyperparameter Tunning

    Tune the hyper-parameters of the model to improve model performance.

    Before you head out on your vacaction, how about some final tweaks to ensure the predicted destination is the indeed the best. You basically be more precise about your preferences such as your ideal budget, your preferred region for vaction and feed this to the software to help it ensure the best possible prediction for you.
